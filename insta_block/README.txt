Instagram Block Module for Drupal 8
------------
The Instagram Block Module provides a fully responsive block that can display any Instagram account with many customisation options such as number of images to show and number of columns to display for different screen sizes.


Installation
------------

1. Unzip insta_block.zip and upload and place it into your Drupal 8 website's /modules directory.

2. Sign in to your site as admin and go to Extend (admin/modules), and activate the ‘Instagram Block' module.
  
3. Go to Structure > Block layout (admin/structure/block) and click 'Place Block' on the desired region (typically under 'Content').

4. In the opened dialog, look for ‘Instagram Block' (insta_block) and click 'Place block'.

5. Configure block with your Instagram Account Name and click 'Save block'
