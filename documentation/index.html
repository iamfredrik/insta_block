What is this?

Instagram Block module for Drupal 8 provides the simplest way to embed your Instagram Account on your Drupal 8 website. It allows you to display any number of posts from any Instagram account using a fully responsive column layout. There are many options such as number of columns to show on each screen size and you can choose if you want to show or hide the image caption. This module also allows you to override the styling using your own custom css, so you can adjust fonts and colors to perfectly fit the layout of your website.
 
Instagram is the number one network for people all over the world to share images. Thus, Instagram is an important platform for promoting your business and products. This module lets you easily embed and promote any Instagram account on your website.
 

Features

• Display posts from any Instagram account in a block
 
• Manage number of posts to show
 
• Fully responsive using Twitter Bootstrap
 
• Optional column count for different screen sizes
 
• Option to show/hide caption for images
 
• Use your own css to customize the style of your posts
 

Install Instagram Block

Installation

------------
 
1. Unzip the package you have downloaded.
 
1. Unzip insta_block.zip and upload and place it into your Drupal 8 website's /modules directory.
 
2. Sign in to your site as admin and go to Extend (admin/modules), and activate the 'Instagram Block' module.
  
3. Go to Structure > Block layout (admin/structure/block) and click 'Place Block' on the desired region (typically under 'Content').
 
4. In the opened dialog, look for 'Instagram Block' (insta_block) and click 'Place block'.
 
5. Configure block with your Instagram Account Name and click 'Save block'

Customisation

Block Settings

------------

You can change some of the settings for this module.

1. Go to Structure > Block layout (admin/structure/block) and look for 'Instagram Block' and click 'Configure'

There you can change:

  • name of instagram account to show
  • number of posts to show
  • number of columns for small devices (tablets, 768px and up)
  • number of columns for medium devices (desktops, 992px and up)
  • number of columns for large devices (large desktops, 1200px and up)
  • hide/show caption

2. Once you have entered your options click 'Save block'
